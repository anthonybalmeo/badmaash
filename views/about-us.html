About Us

Badmaash does indian food in a cool-casual way. We serve casual and traditional indian flavors with DTLA swag - with beer, wine, and all that goodstuff! we source local, organic and sustainable as much as we can… but it’s hard to find kala-namak in Cali. Oh, and we have a real chef. like REAL real. Over 40 years in the kitch, with numerous awards and accolades – Chef Pav kicks ass – no more greasy, over-spiced, mystery dish that you cant even pronounce… who eats that trash anyway?

Menu

OUR FOOD IS #BADASS

Badmaash is not your typical indian food. We try to bring flavors from India and celebrate them here. You can describe us as a mashup of Punjabi, French, and Canadian cuisine.
